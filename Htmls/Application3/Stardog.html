Stardog

StarDog and TurboCat is a 2019 British 3D computer-animated superhero film produced by Head Gear Films and Screen Yorkshire and distributed by Kaleidoscope Film Distribution and Viva Kids. The film was animated by British animation studio Red Star 3D. Originally titled SpaceDog and TurboCat,[2] the film was directed and written by Ben Smith and features the voices of Nick Frost, Luke Evans, Gemma Arterton, Bill Nighy, Ben Bailey Smith, Charli D'Amelio and Cory English.
